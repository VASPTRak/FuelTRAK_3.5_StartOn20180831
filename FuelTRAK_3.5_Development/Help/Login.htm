A Username and Password are required to access FuelTRAK.
  1. Open your Internet Browser.
  2. Input the http address for your FuelTRAK software press the Enter key on the keyboard. (Example: http://www.mycompany.fueltrak.com).
  3. The Login screen will load in the internet browser.
  4. A Username and Password are required to access FuelTrak.  If you do not have a Username and Password please contact IT Support.
  5. Enter your User Name. (Example: Harrison).
  6. Enter your Password. (Example: log1in).
  7. Click the Login button.
